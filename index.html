VocabBoost

I proposed a vocabulary-enhancing app for diverse users, including students (school, college, ESL learners), working professionals (marketing, managers, writers), job seekers, and parents. Key features include clear word definitions, practical usage examples, user progress tracking, and interactive exercises. The app aims to boost academic success, professional communication, job readiness, and early language development. Parents with limited English proficiency can also improve their language skills. Engaging quizzes further reinforce vocabulary retention and practical application.
